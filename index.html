Billy Ferguson

Hello! I am a first-year Economics PhD student at the Stanford Graduate School of Business. My current interests are in the foundations of choice, public economics, and causal inference. My previous work has leveraged GPS data to study dynamic racial segregation in cities and the efficacy of shelter-in-place policy responses to the SARS-CoV-2 pandemic.

I grew up in Kansas (yes, there's no place like home) and studied math in my undergrad at Stanford University.
